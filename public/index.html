Projects

Surf & Paddle

In surf and paddle, I was challenged to build a marketing page complete with blog posts, block quotes, an ad, social buttons, and other various other styling parameters. One point of keen interest was keeping the page's styling consistent and on-brand. There needed to be overlays and hover selectors, as well as a limited color palette comprising subtle warm tones.

Surf and paddle is a non-profit started by two Bay Area surfers, Lil and Lily John, who are on a mission to bring peace to the beach through lifestyle and etiquette courses focused on a minimal carbon footprint and fire safety.

T Shirt Shop

The focus of the T Shirt Shop project was implementing a clean grid layout and media queries in a storefront context. After getting the grid in place, I then used Flexbox to organize the buttons along the bottom. It was also necessary to give the user options of shirt color and size, which I accomplished with a hover dropdown menu.

After completing the styling, I added a media query to make the site responsive to tablets and mobile devices. The media query causes the grid to shift to a single colum and rearranges the elements inside each grid item to a horizontal layout.
